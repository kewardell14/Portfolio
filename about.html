About

Hi, I'm Katelyn!

Thank You For Reviewing My Portfolio

I'm a Graphic Designer with a passion for Branding and Packaging. With knowledge in Web Design and UX/UI, I aspire to create the best brand experience possible by combining all my design knowledge.

I think of myself as a craftsman in all that I do. One of my main creative outlets is crochet, which has helped me to develop a passion for hand making and crafts. I love seeing a project come together, especially from scratch.

While design absolutely should look good, I believe that design should convey messages that improve the functionality of everyday things.

An ambition of mine is to someday be involved in designing an escape room experience, combining my love for storytelling, design, and puzzles. I discovered my love for escape rooms at a young age, admiring the pairing of themes, logic, and design to create an immersive experience that creates amazing memories for all who participate.

I love to learn new things about design and Adobe, especially when it is a trick that can save me hours of work! I love learning from others and always appreciate a chance to find ways to improve my work.

I believe in doing a job right, no shortcuts. My strong moral compass and dedication to my work comes from my upbringing and my experiences in TaeKwondo, where putting in the work and effort is just as important as the end result.

In addition to crochet and participating in escape rooms, I enjoy playing the piano, reading, playing board games, playing video games, and spending time with my family.
