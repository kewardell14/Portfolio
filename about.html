About

Hi, I'm Katelyn!

Thank You For Reviewing My Portfolio

I’m a Graphic Designer who loves branding and packaging, and I use my background in web design and UX/UI to build brand experiences that feel thoughtful and genuine.

I’ve always approached design like a craft. Crochet taught me to enjoy slow, hands-on work and to appreciate the moment when everything clicks together. I bring that same mindset to design. It should look good, of course, but it should also make things clearer, easier, and more meaningful.

I care about doing things the right way. That comes from my upbringing and from TaeKwondo, where effort and consistency matter just as much as the final result.

Outside of design, I’m usually crocheting, solving escape rooms, playing the piano, reading, gaming, or hanging out with my family.
