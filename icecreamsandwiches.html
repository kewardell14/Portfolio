Project Overview

This project focuses on redesigning the packaging for a single product across three distinct audiences: kids ages 7–10, college students, and seniors 65+. Through empathic research and visual exploration, I identified how age, lifestyle, and usability needs influence packaging expectations.

I developed three audience-specific packaging solutions that align structure, typography, and messaging while remaining functional as three-dimensional objects.

Client:

Kinder, Hershey's, and Pepperidge Farms

Disciplines:

Branding, Digital, Packaging, Social Media, Photography
