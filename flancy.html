About

Create a font, including all the major glyphs. This should include 26 upper case, 26 lowercase, 10 Numbers, and 35 Punctuation. All the glyphs should have a sense of unity and flow well together when typed out into words. In addition, create a poster displaying the font.

Disciplines:

Typography

Flancy is a sans serif typeface, with script embellishments.

Both fancy, and laid back, Flancy is a font that elegant, but not too elegant, and simple, but not too simple, creating a fun and playful typeface.

Icons

Whenever possible, the icons should be constructed using pieces of the font characters from the typeface "Urbane Rounded Bold." This helps to give the icons a cohesive look that ties them to the overall brand identity. The other icons should follow the same organic style, using thick lines and rounded edges.
