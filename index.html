Welcome to my Portfolio!

Hi there! I am currently a Graphic Design student at BYU-Idaho, with a minor in Web Design. My emphasis is in Branding and Identity, but I have also dabbled in UX/UI. I love exploring how design can effectively communicate messages and connect with audiences. My academic journey and work experiences have helped me develop a strong work ethic and attention to detail. Through my experiences working on team projects such as the Outlet Publication, the Flood 50 campaign for the city of Rexburg, and rebranding the College Performing and Visual Arts through my position at BYU-Idaho, I have been able to develop my skills as a collaborative designer.
