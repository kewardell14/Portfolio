About

Create a cohesive, cost-effective menu, logotype, and receipt for a single location sit-down restaurant.

The goal is to develop a visually consistent system, without photography or excessive illustration, that reflects the restaurant’s character, stays affordable to reprint each season, and appeals to mid-range diners similar to those at Outback Steak house, Pei Wei, or Carrabba’s Italian Grill.
